# Iso LOuca — Pendrive do Tecnico

Conteudo deste pendrive:
  ISOs\         Sistemas bootaveis (Windows, Linux, Recovery)
  Tools\        Programas portateis
  Drivers\      Snappy Driver Installer + pacotes OEM
  Scripts\      Utilitarios de manutencao
  MS Office setup\  Instalador Office (cliente ativa depois)

Inicio rapido:
  1. Boot: selecione uma ISO no menu Ventoy
  2. No Windows: abra Scripts\Menu-Tecnico.ps1
  3. Office: MS Office setup\scripts\04-Instalar-Office.ps1

Links uteis:
  Ventoy:     https://www.ventoy.net
  Win11 ISO:  https://www.microsoft.com/software-download/windows11
  Win10 ISO:  https://www.microsoft.com/software-download/windows10
